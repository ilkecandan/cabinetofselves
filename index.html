What is Internal Family Systems (IFS)?

Internal Family Systems (IFS) is a powerfully transformative, evidence-based psychotherapy model developed by Richard C. Schwartz, Ph.D. It offers a clear, non-pathologizing, and empowering way of understanding human consciousness.

IFS suggests that our minds are naturally multiple, not just as a result of trauma or mental illness, but as a normal and healthy aspect of human experience. We all have an "internal family" of parts—sub-personalities with their own unique qualities, beliefs, and emotions.

The Importance of Visualization

In IFS, visualizing your internal parts can significantly deepen your understanding and connection with them. Cabinet of Selves provides an intuitive interface to help you do just that.

By creating visual representations, you can more easily identify, communicate with, and ultimately heal your inner landscape. This process makes abstract psychological concepts tangible and actionable.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Cabinet of Selves and IFS.

Is Cabinet of Selves a substitute for therapy?

No, Cabinet of Selves is a self-help tool designed to complement therapeutic work, not replace it. It can be a great adjunct to IFS therapy or a resource for personal exploration.

Do I need to know IFS to use this app?

Not at all! Our app is designed to introduce IFS concepts in an accessible way, with guided exercises and clear explanations for beginners.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. We prioritize your privacy and data security. All your information is encrypted and stored securely. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for more details.

What devices is the app compatible with?

Cabinet of Selves is a Progressive Web App (PWA), meaning it works across all modern browsers on desktop and mobile devices. You can install it directly from your browser.
